1. Introduction

Sri Sports Events ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us in any way.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing information, and other details provided when you register for an event or service.
  • Event Information: Details related to your event preferences, ticket purchases, and participation.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and other details automatically collected when you visit our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services, including event registration, management, and customer support.
  • To communicate with you regarding your event registration, updates, and promotional offers.
  • To improve our services, website, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in providing our services, such as payment processors, email communication services, and IT support.
  • Legal Compliance: Authorities or legal entities if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
